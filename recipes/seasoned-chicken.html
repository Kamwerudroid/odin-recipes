Description

This is a simple recipe that can be made in under an hour.

The recipe requires very little preparation and can be altered to the taste of the person making it or the people who will be eating this dish. It is simple enough that inexperienced people can make the dish.

The chicken is best served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 300 grams chicken breast
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • parsley
  • 1 tea spoon turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Olive oil
  • 1 tea spoon Salt
  • 2 cups Water

Steps

  1. Chop the onions
  2. Pour the Olive oil into a pan
  3. Put the onions in the pan with olive oil
  4. Cut the tomatoes, garlic, parsley and mix together
  5. Fry the onions until golden brown
  6. Place the cut vegetables into the frying onions and fry until the tomatoes are soft
  7. Add the spices and salt
  8. Place the chicken into the pan and fry
  9. Lower the heat and add one cup of water and allow to simmer
